Competitive Set Report

Track your cowork's performance data over time in a neutral and objective manner. Compare your cowork’s performance to your competitors. The report also provides a deeper look into your cowork's monthly performance and how it ranks to the ones of your comp set. The Comp Set helps you 1. before launch: analyse the expected profitability 2. during operations: see how your competitors perform by segment 3. at exit: provide due diligence documents to the buyer.

Features

  • Your cowork’s performance compared to the performance of your competitive set
  • Monthly Occupancy, Monthly Rates and Revenue Per Available Desk (RevPAR)
  • Data for the most recent month and the last twenty-four months if available
  • Segmentation by Services: Flex Desks, Fixed Desks, Teams and Meeting Rooms.
  • Monthly availability

Trend Report

Whether you would like to value a space, acquire a new property or generally consult a wide-range of data on a specific area, Trend Reports are the ideal resource to conduct a thorough analysis of a new location.

Features

  • No individual cowork's data is displayed in a Trend Report.
  • Report metrics include Occupancy, Revenue per Desk, Revenue per Available Desk, revenue, supply and demand with year-over-year percentage changes.
  • Custom Trends are available to measure performance for a specified set of coworks.
  • Multiple options are available for report currency.
